### Polished Version

"remain stuck at traditional working speeds"

"AI will significantly enhance productivity."

Advanced Vocabulary

External image

calm, composed, reliable

Internal reality

### Polished Version

> If I knew for certain that I only had five minutes to talk to my 30-year-old self, I probably wouldn't ask very deep or complicated questions. ### Improvements

English Corrections
❌ "If i know for a fact"

✅ Better:

"If I knew for a fact..."

Because you're imagining a hypothetical situation, English uses the past form (knew) even though you're talking about the future.

❌ "If married or dating who that person is?"

✅ Better:

"If you're married or dating someone, who is that person?"

or

"Are you married or dating anyone?"

❌ "how really are you"

✅ Better:

"How are you, really?"

This is a very natural expression.

It implies:

"I want the honest answer, not the polite one."

A Vocabulary Upgrade

Instead of:

"disastrous events"

You could say:

"major setbacks"

or

"significant challenges"

For example:

I would want to know whether there are any significant challenges ahead and how I overcome them.

That sounds more mature and less dramatic.
