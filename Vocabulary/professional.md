# Professional Vocabulary

## Collaboration

### Meaning

Working effectively with others.

### Example

Collaboration is essential in software development.

### My Sentence

I learned collaboration while working as a tour leader.

---

## Initiative

### Meaning

The ability to act without being told.

### Example

Employers value people who show initiative.

### My Sentence

I took the initiative to solve the problem during our Manali trip.

---

## Accountability

### Meaning

Taking responsibility for outcomes.

### Example

Good leaders demonstrate accountability.

### My Sentence

As a class representative, I learned accountability.
