# Daily Use Vocabulary

## To Be Honest

### Meaning

Used to introduce a sincere opinion.

### Example

To be honest, I was nervous before the interview.

---

## Looking Forward To

### Meaning

Feeling excited about something in the future.

### Example

I am looking forward to starting my first job.

---

## Figure Out

### Meaning

To understand or solve something.

### Example

I am trying to figure out which backend path suits me best.
